About us

This website offers ride-sharing services between classmates of E-business master winter intake 2019. The platform matches between classmates who have cars and want to share the ride in return for a fee. The platform, called EbussCar, will have predefined meeting points in various parts of the city, called Locations; and predefined arriving points around the CBS campus, called Destinations.

Price

The price of the route will consist of Default Price that is a revenue for EbussCar and a Profit Margin which will be a profit for a Driver for sharing the car.

Cancellation fee

A Cancellation Fee has a detering function to discourage cancellations. If, despite this, cancellation happens, the User will have an option to Report the other User with automatic consequence of getting a 1 star rating for a ride.
